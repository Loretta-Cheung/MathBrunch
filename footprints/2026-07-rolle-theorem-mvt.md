# 2026-07 — Rolle's Theorem → Mean Value Theorem

## What happened

A basic theorem in calculus became emotionally memorable because it described a structural inevitability: under certain conditions, a point with zero derivative must exist.

## Why it mattered

This made calculus feel less like mechanical differentiation and more like a language for constraints.
